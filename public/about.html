Our Story

From the coast to your plate.

A Modern Classic

Founded in 2025, Fish n Chips was born from a desire to elevate the traditional British takeaway. We noticed that while the world was moving forward, the local chippy was stuck in the past.

We source our fish from sustainable stocks in the North Atlantic, ensuring that we're not just feeding you today, but protecting the oceans for tomorrow. Our potatoes are grown less than 50 miles away, supporting local farmers and reducing our carbon footprint.

But it's not just about the ingredients. It's about the experience. We've stripped away the grease and the grime to create a dining experience that feels fresh, modern, and inviting.
